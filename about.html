About Us

Our Mission

We are dedicated to providing high-quality vocational education and technical training to 10th and 12th-grade pass students. Our primary goal is to bridge the gap between industry requirements and student skills, ensuring that our youth are fully prepared for successful careers in various industrial and technical sectors.

Trades Offered

We offer specialized, hands-on training in highly demanded industrial trades. Our workshops are fully equipped to provide practical knowledge in:

  • Electrician: Comprehensive training in industrial and domestic electrical wiring, machinery operations, and safety protocols.
  • Fitter: Expert guidance on assembling, manufacturing, and maintaining mechanical components and heavy machinery.

Why Choose Us?

With experienced faculty, modern workshops, and a strong focus on practical learning, we ensure that every student who graduates from our institute is industry-ready and capable of securing excellent employment opportunities or starting their own venture.
